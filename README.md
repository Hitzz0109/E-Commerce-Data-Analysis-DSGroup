---

## Key Findings

- 📉 **18.7%** of all orders are losing money
- 💸 **$135,376** profit destroyed by excessive discounting
- ⚠️ Profit turns **negative at 30%+ discount**
- 🗺️ **Central region** has worst margin at 7.92%
- 📦 **Tables** sub-category losing **-$17,725**

---

## Strategic Recommendations

| # | Recommendation | Expected Impact |
|---|---|---|
| R1 | Cap all discounts at 20% | Recover ~$81K |
| R2 | Emergency audit of Central region | Fix 31.9% loss orders |
| R3 | Stop discounting Tables & Bookcases | Save $21K+ |
| R4 | VIP customer retention programme | Protect 17.5% of profit |
| R5 | Invest in Technology category | Leverage 17.4% margin |
